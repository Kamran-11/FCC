Here is a selected timeline of Bruce Lee's life:

  • 1940 - Bruce was born
  • 1952 - Bruce starts school
  • 1954 - Bruce begins his training
  • 1959 - Bruce moves to America
  • 1960 - Bruce graduates from High School
  • 1961 - Bruce at University
  • 1964 - Controversial private match with Wong Jack Man
  • 1967 - Played Kato in The Green Hornet
  • 1971 - Four episodes of Longstreet
  • 1973 - Bruce Lee passed away
